About Oakline

Oakline is a small clothing brand built in Okotoks, we focus on clean graphics, and pieces that feel good now and still look good in 10 years.

We focus on heavier fabrics, simple designs, and a calm, old-money aesthetic — the kind of stuff you can wear to school, out, or on a trip and never feel out of place.
